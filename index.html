Valorant Guide for Beginners

What is Valorant?

Riot Games's first ever FPS (first person shooter) game, Valorant, sister to Riot's most succesful franchise 'League of Legends', was released in 2020. It is described as a 5v5 character-based tactical FPS where precise gunplay meets unique agent abilities. Two teams, defenders vs attackers, play to win 13 rounds on various maps. Valorant is the perfect blend between CSGO and Overwatch, blending exciting agent abilities with traditional gun-play to bring in new dimensional gameplays.There's much to learn, but fret not! Valorant is a fairly simple and user friendly FPS game that is quick to pick up. This guide will cover all the basics you need to know to get started!
