Proposal for Project

The website will be about Riot's first FPS game, Valorant. The objective of the website is to serve as a friendly guide for beginners to the game, especially if they are unfamiliar with FPS games. I want to tailor it to the female demographic as many girls find it too daunting to start gaming in a heavily male dominated server. But I hope to ease the barrier of entry to gaming for girls as it's important that the gaming landscape can be more inclusive to everyone.

Content I plan to include in each page

  • Objectives of the Game (Attackers vs Defenders)
  • FPS Basics (Movement, Crosshair, Sensitivity)
  • Agent Characters - Ability Explanation
  • Maps
  • Game Modes and Ranking
  • Glossary
